    About

    About Us

    At Biryani Chowk, we believe that great food isn't just about taste — it's about creating memories, bringing people together, and celebrating the joy of sharing a flavorful meal. Born from a deep-rooted love for authentic barbecue, our journey started with a simple mission: to serve bold, smoky, and unforgettable dishes that satisfy the soul as much as the appetite.

    We take pride in our commitment to tradition, using time-honored smoking techniques combined with fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Every cut of meat is slow-cooked to perfection, every side is made with love, and our signature sauces? Crafted in-house with just the right balance of tang, spice, and sweet.

    Whether you're grabbing a quick bite, enjoying a relaxed family dinner, or hosting a full-blown backyard bash, Biryani Chowk delivers the same passion and flavor every time. Dine in with us, take it to-go, or let us cater your special occasions — we’re here to fire up your taste buds and create a true barbecue experience.

    Come hungry, leave happy. Biryani Chowk isn't just a place to eat — it's where barbecue dreams come to life.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Can I order BBQ food for delivery?

    Yes, we offer home delivery through in-house staff and platforms like Zomato, Swiggy, and Uber Eats.

    What are the hours?

    Our hours of operation are every day from 6 PM to 11 PM.

    Do you have vegetarian BBQ options?

    Yes, we offer delicious vegetarian BBQ options such as paneer tikka, mushroom skewers, and grilled vegetables.

    Do you offer a kids' menu?

    Yes, we have a kid-friendly BBQ menu with smaller portions and milder flavors.

    Do you offer vegetarian sides with the BBQ?

    Yes, we offer a variety of vegetarian sides such as coleslaw, salads, fries, and cornbread.
